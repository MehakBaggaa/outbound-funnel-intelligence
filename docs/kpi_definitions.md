# KPI Definitions

This document defines the key performance indicators used in the Outbound Funnel Intelligence project. All metrics are calculated at the journey level, where a journey is defined as a unique combination of Email + Campaign.

## Funnel Performance KPIs

### Reply Rate

Measures the percentage of outreach journeys that received a response.

Formula:
Reply Rate = Replied Journeys / Total Journeys × 100

### Negative Response Rate

Measures the percentage of outreach journeys that received a negative response.

Formula:
Negative Response Rate = Negative Response Journeys / Total Journeys × 100

### Reply-to-Qualification Rate

Measures how effectively replies are converted into qualified opportunities.

Formula:
Reply-to-Qualification Rate = Qualified Journeys / Replied Journeys × 100

### Qualified-to-Conversion Rate

Measures how effectively qualified opportunities convert into customers.

Formula:
Qualified-to-Conversion Rate = Converted Journeys / Qualified Journeys × 100

### Overall Conversion Rate

Measures the percentage of total outreach journeys that resulted in conversion.

Formula:
Overall Conversion Rate = Converted Journeys / Total Journeys × 100

---

## Sender Overlap KPIs

### Sender Overlap Rate

Measures the percentage of unique human identities contacted by more than one sender.

Formula:
Sender Overlap Rate = Overlapped Humans / Total Unique Humans × 100

### Reply Rate by Overlap Status

Compares reply performance between overlapped and non-overlapped audiences.

### Qualification Rate by Overlap Status

Compares qualification performance between overlapped and non-overlapped audiences.

### Conversion Rate by Overlap Status

Compares conversion performance between overlapped and non-overlapped audiences.

### Average Touchpoints by Overlap Status

Measures average outreach volume for overlapped versus non-overlapped audiences.

---

## Touchpoint Effectiveness KPIs

### Conversion Rate by Touchpoint Count

Measures how conversion performance changes as the number of outreach touchpoints increases.

### Negative Response Rate by Touchpoint Count

Measures how negative response behavior changes as outreach frequency increases.

---

## Outreach Efficiency KPIs

### Average Outreach Duration

Measures the average number of days between the first and last outreach touchpoint.

### Average Gap Between Touchpoints

Measures the average number of days between outreach attempts.

### Conversion Rate by Outreach Pattern

Evaluates how outreach duration and touchpoint cadence impact conversion performance.

---

## CRM Data Quality KPIs

### CRM Duplication Rate

Measures the percentage of CRM records that represent duplicate human identities.

Formula:
CRM Duplication Rate = (CRM Records − Unique Human Identities) / CRM Records × 100

### Duplicate Identity Prevalence

Measures the proportion of CRM records flagged as duplicate identities.

### Conversion Rate by Identity Type

Compares conversion performance between duplicate and non-duplicate identities.

### Campaign Exposure by Identity Type

Measures average campaign exposure for duplicate versus non-duplicate identities.

### Sender Overlap Rate by Identity Type

Measures overlap prevalence across different identity groups.

---

## Response Quality KPIs

### Qualification Rate by Response Stage

Measures the percentage of journeys that become qualified opportunities for each response stage.

### Conversion Rate by Response Stage

Measures the percentage of journeys that convert for each response stage.

These metrics help identify which response types are most predictive of successful outcomes.
